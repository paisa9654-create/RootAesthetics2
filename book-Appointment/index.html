Booking FAQs

How long does it take to confirm my appointment?

We aim to confirm all appointments within 24 hours of receiving your booking email. You'll receive a confirmation email with all the details.

Can I book for someone else?

Yes, you can book an appointment for someone else. Please include their name and contact details in the "Additional Notes" section of the booking email.

What happens if I need to cancel or reschedule?

If you need to cancel or reschedule, please reply to your confirmation email or contact us at info@rootaesthetics.co.uk at least 48 hours before your appointment.

Is the consultation fee paid in advance?

No, the £50 consultation fee is payable at the clinic during your appointment and is fully deductible from any treatment you choose to proceed with.
