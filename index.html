About the Specialist

Dr. Anju Mehta

MBBS, MD, Level 7 Diploma

Dr. Anju Mehta brings more than 20 years of clinical experience as an Anaesthetist working in the NHS and internationally to her aesthetics practice.

She ventured into the field of Aesthetics after completing the Ofqual-regulated and JCCP-approved postgraduate Level 7 diploma in injectables from one of the leading providers and now works exclusively in the aesthetics sector.

Her Philosophy
"She takes a highly personalised and ethical approach to treating all her patients, ensuring a natural rejuvenated look."

Dr. Mehta is an extremely skilled medical aesthetics doctor who has a special interest in regenerative medicine. Her calm and composed demeanour along with meticulous attention to detail makes her very popular with all her patients thus creating long term patient journeys.

Committed to continuous professional development, Dr. Anju Mehta stays abreast of the latest advancements in aesthetic medicine to provide cutting-edge treatments with the highest standards of care.
